### RHUDS Theme

```
Colors:
- Primary: #29F2DF (Cyan)
- Secondary: #1C7FA6 (Blue)
- Accent: #EF3EF1 (Magenta)
- Background: #0a0a0a (Deep Black)
- Text: #ffffff (White)

Design:
- Border Radius: 4px (smooth)
- Border: 1px solid
- Grid: Solid lines
- Glow: Subtle effects
```

### Cold War Theme

```
Colors:
- Primary: #FFB000 (Tactical Amber)
- Secondary: #33FF00 (Phosphor Green)
- Accent: #FF3333 (Muted Red)
- Background: #0a0a0c (Deep Black)
- Text: #FFB000 (Amber)

Design:
- Border Radius: 0px (sharp)
- Border: 2px solid with glow
- Grid: Dashed lines
- Glow: Intense effects
- Corner Markers: Tactical indicators
```
