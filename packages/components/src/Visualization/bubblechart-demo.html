About RHUDS Bubble Charts

The RHUDS theme provides a modern, clean aesthetic with smooth transitions and subtle effects.

  • Color Palette: Cyan (#29F2DF), Blue (#1C7FA6), Magenta (#EF3EF1)
  • Design: Smooth rounded corners, subtle grid lines, clean typography
  • Use Case: Modern dashboards, analytics, business intelligence

ABOUT COLD WAR BUBBLE CHARTS

The Cold War theme provides a tactical, military aesthetic with intense colors and glow effects.

  • COLOR PALETTE: Tactical Amber (#FFB000), Phosphor Green (#33FF00), Muted Red (#FF3333)
  • DESIGN: Sharp angular corners, dashed grid lines, monospace typography
  • USE CASE: Tactical analysis, military operations, strategic planning
